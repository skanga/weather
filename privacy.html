Privacy Policy

No cookies. No tracking. No ads.

This site does not use cookies, does not track your location, and does not collect any personal information. We don't use analytics, fingerprinting, or any form of user tracking.

When you search for a city or zip code, the coordinates of that location (not your personal location) are sent to the following third-party services to retrieve weather data:

  • Open-Meteo — weather forecasts and air quality data
  • National Weather Service (NWS) — severe weather alerts (US only)
  • RainViewer — radar imagery
  • OpenWeatherMap — severe weather alerts for non-US, non-European locations (via our alerts-proxy)
  • Google Pollen API — pollen data (only when you click "See pollen data")
  • Zippopotam — postal/zip code to coordinates (60+ countries)
  • CartoDB — map tiles for radar display

Your preferences (dark mode, temperature units, time format, and section layout) are saved in your browser's local storage (not a cookie) so they persist between visits. Pollen data is also cached locally to reduce unnecessary API calls. You can clear all of this anytime by clearing your browser data.

Your phone might ask you to "Install this page", which means creating a shortcut to a website on your home screen, known as a Progressive Web App (PWA). This makes the site act like a native app, allowing it to load faster, and appear with your other apps on your home screen.

This site is open source and supported by tips from our visitors. No data is sold, shared, or monetized in any way. If you think this is awesome, please consider clicking here to support us.
